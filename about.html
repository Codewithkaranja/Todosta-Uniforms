Our heritage

Craftsmanship since 2010

Todosta Uniforms began in a small workshop in Machakos with a simple vision: to create uniforms that combine durability with elegance. What started as a family-run atelier serving local schools has grown into a trusted name for bespoke tailoring across Kenya.

Our founder, a master tailor with over 20 years of experience, instilled a philosophy that still guides us today: every stitch matters, every client is a partner, and quality is never compromised. We've built lasting relationships with schools, hotels, corporations, and individuals who share our passion for excellence.

"We don't just make uniforms – we help institutions express their identity. Whether it's a school crest embroidered on a blazer or a hotel's brand colours woven into a waistcoat, we bring your vision to life."

— Tobias Makau
Founder & Master Tailor

Our Process

From consultation to delivery, every step is personal and precise.

1

Consultation

2

Design & sampling
